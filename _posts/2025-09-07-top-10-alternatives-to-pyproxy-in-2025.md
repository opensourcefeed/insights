## 1. ProxyEmpire
Proxy Empire is at the top of the list in case you are serious about proxies. A unique feature of this tool is the large size of the IP pool (more than 9.5 million rotating IPs), sophisticated targeting (city, region, ISP), and special features such as rollover data. The pricing at [ProxyEmpire](https://proxyempire.io/proxyempire-vs-pyproxy/) is quite flexible, and the support is excellent—24-hour API access and unlimited concurrent connections are something I appreciate. Their high ethical position (EWDCI certification) is also crucial to those who require adherence and trustworthiness. 

## 2. Decodo (formerly Smartproxy)
Decodo has come a long way and has an impressive proxy pool with over 125 million IP addresses located worldwide, including residential and mobile proxies. Their dashboard is user-friendly and free of clutter, hence presenting them as one of the best options that one can use. Large-scale organizations as well as single businesses find Decodo useful, especially with regard to large data collection and effective data management. 

## 3. NetNut
The main reasons I was attracted to NetNut are the direct ISP connectivity that offers low latency and a consistently stable web scraping, SEO, or even market research. Its massive 85+ million residential IPs with 195 countries and an intuitive dashboard make it a juggernaut of scale and performance.

## 4. Bright Data
Bright Data (formerly Luminati) defines the standards in the industry of proxies. They can provide an ideal combination of residential, data center, ISP, and mobile proxies, whether you need to collect public web data, conduct ad verification, or do price monitoring projects. They are popular amongst data professionals because of the robust API access.

## 5. Oxylabs
Oxylabs is complemented by its widespread network and high-quality reliability. I adore their high-powered dashboard and service coverage—195+ countries and advanced scraping tools. Their offerings will include residential, datacenter, and mobile proxies, feature-rich solutions that will ensure it makes block-free data collection.

## 6. IPRoyal
Customers focused on cost-efficient operations without compromising reliability will find it hard to beat IPRoyal. It is among the best performers, offering unlimited traffic, supporting SOCKS5, and innovative pay-as-you-go billing, making it ideal for businesses seeking affordable and scalable proxies.

## 7. SOAX
The key advantage of SOAX is flexibility: a large network of proxies with convenient filtering (city, ASN) and support of most protocols and rotation schemes. Their pricing layout and around-the-clock support make them very affordable to both novices and professionals.

## 8. Apify
Apify is a real game-changer when priority is given to automation and web scraping. They also offer their open-source software and already-made templates to start scraping the web in a matter of minutes. They have a seamless proxy integration, which is handy when one needs to carry out complex automation and data extraction procedures.

## 9. ProxyJet
ProxyJet provides rotating residential, fixed, mobile, and datacenter proxies with 75+ million IPs in 180+ countries. They bill only when a connection is successful (no charge in case of failure), making pricing clear and attractive. The dashboard is user-friendly with self-service, allowing instant activation.

## 10. ASocks
ASocks is rapidly gaining popularity in the provision of unlimited residential proxies at an affordable rate. Their worldwide coverage (more than 150 countries) and their pay-for-traffic model are suitable for entry-level users or those who want to minimize initial commitment.
