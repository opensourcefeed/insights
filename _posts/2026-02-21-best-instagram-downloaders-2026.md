## Legal & Ethical Considerations When Downloading Instagram Videos

Before using any Instagram downloader, remember that most content on Instagram is protected by copyright and remains the property of its original creator. Downloading videos for personal offline viewing may be acceptable in some contexts, but redistributing, modifying, or using someone else’s content for commercial purposes without permission can violate copyright laws and Instagram’s Terms of Service. To stay safe and ethical, always seek permission before reposting, provide proper credit, and avoid downloading private or restricted content.
